What is the hit die for each class in D&D 5e?

Different classes use different hit dice: Barbarians (d12), Fighters/Paladins/Rangers (d10), Bards/Clerics/Druids/Monks/Rogues/Warlocks (d8), and Sorcerers/Wizards (d6).

Should I roll for HP or take the average?

Taking the average provides consistent results (higher than the mathematical average due to D&D's rounding rule), while rolling offers the chance for higher HP at the risk of lower results. Many players prefer the reliability of taking the average.
